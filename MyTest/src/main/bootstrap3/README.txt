#####################################
Assumptions
#####################################
1. Only Two teams can play
2. Play list are pre loaded. 
3. Songs in the playlist are preloaded

#####################################
Menu
#####################################
1. Choose Playlist
2. Setup Team 1
2. Setup Team 2

#####################################
Actions
#####################################
Setup 
	Setting Team - Enter Team Name, Icon and Color
	Setting Play - Choose Time for Timer Clock
				 - Select First team to start
				 - Choose the play list from the collection. 
Load the new page
	Click-able play, pause 
	Ability to track score points with songs
	Ability to track bonus points with songs
	Ability to show the answers 
	Increment the score. 

#####################################
Panels
#####################################
Team Panel
- Show team total score
- Show total number of songs

Song Panel
- Show Song Name for confirmation
- Show Clickable Prev, Play, Replay, Pause, Next buttons
- Show timer clock
